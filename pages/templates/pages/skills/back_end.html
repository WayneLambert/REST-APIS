Back End Web Developer Skills

The objective of this page is to answer questions relating to the skills I have in relation to back-end development.

I have often been asked to rate myself in a particular skill between 1 and 10. This is not a helpful way to judge a skill since a rating between 1 and 10 can be thought of in many different ways. Amongst other ways of thinking about this scale, a 10 score could either be "knowing the sum of all knowledge relating to a skill" or "being the best in the world at a particular skill".

The two measures are a mile off one another. The person with the most knowledge in the world of a skill in a vast subject area, let's say Python, might only score a 6 in terms of "knowing the sum of all knowledge" relating to Python. There is simply too much to know. There are over 240,000 projects released on PyPI solving many problems across many sectors, therefore it is impossible to "know the sum of all knowledge".
